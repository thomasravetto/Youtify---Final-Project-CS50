What is Youtify?

Have you ever wanted to transfer songs from a Youtube playlist to Spotify but gave up because it would be too boring and tiring? Well, if this is your case Youtify will help you: this app transfers all the songs (or the majority) from one service to another. To try it out head to the Transfer section.



How does it work?

Youtify is a Flask based application, functioning is simple, for every song in the playlist, the app takes the title and artist's name and add it to Spotify.
For further information, please head to the About section.
