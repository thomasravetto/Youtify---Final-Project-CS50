How to transfer playlists.

- First, paste the URL of the playlist, only the part after "list=" is needed, but if you paste more, don't worry! The app will take care for you, make sure to copy the entire string.



- Second, name your playlist and set if you want your playlist to be public or not, if you don't choose any name the default will be New playlist.


- Third, click on and enjoy as your playlist gets uploaded on your profile!


Keep in mind.

This app extracts information from each video's title, so there may not always be a match on Spotify, to maximize the chances, try to use songs' official music videos as often as possible, in order to avoid titles that can not be analysed.
